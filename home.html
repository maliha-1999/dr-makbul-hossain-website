ABOUT

I am Dr. Md. Mokbul Hossain, currently serving as Consultant Pediatric Cardiologist at Evercare Hospital. Over the years, I have treated countless patients with a commitment to advanced medical practice, research, and patient well-being.

Education & Credentials

Professor Dr. Md. Mokbul Hossain is a child specialist, neonatologist, and pediatric cardiologist from Bangladesh. He has professional practices expanding over a wide area of child health that includes newborn health, childhood illness, neonatal and pediatric emergencies, neonatal and pediatric echocardiography. He has years of experience in treating newborn and children.
