FAQ

What age group of children does Dr. Md. Mokbul Hosen treat?

Dr. Mokbul Hossen provides medical care for newborns, infants, toddlers, and children up to 18 years of age.

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

Yes, it is recommended to book an appointment in advance to avoid waiting. You can schedule easily through the Appointment page or by contacting the clinic directly.

Does doctor provide vaccination and immunization services?

Absolutely. Dr. Mokbul Hossen ensures that children receive all essential vaccinations and provides parents with updated immunization schedules.

How can I prepare my child for their first visit?

Bring your child’s medical records, vaccination card, and a list of any ongoing health issues. Keeping your child calm with their favorite toy or book is also helpful.

Does doctor offer consultation for child nutrition and growth?

Yes. Dr. Hossen guides parents on proper nutrition, growth milestones, and early detection of developmental concerns.

Do you provide additional support?

Call and email support is available 24/7. Phone lines are open during normal business hours.
